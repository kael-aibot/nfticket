# NFTicket Mobile Quick Start Guide

**Quick guide for using NFTicket on mobile devices**

---

## 📱 For Event Attendees (Buyer App)

### Option 1: Web Browser (No Installation)
1. Open your mobile browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android)
2. Visit the deployed NFTicket buyer app URL
3. Browse events, connect your wallet, and buy tickets
4. Your tickets are stored in your wallet!

### Option 2: Add to Home Screen (App-like Experience)

**iOS (Safari):**
1. Open the buyer app in Safari
2. Tap the "Share" button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name it "NFTicket" and tap "Add"
5. Now it launches like a native app!

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the buyer app in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Name it "NFTicket" and tap "Add"
4. Icon appears on your home screen

### Getting Started
1. **Connect Wallet** (recommended): Install Phantom or Solflare wallet app
   - Phantom: phantom.app
   - Solflare: solflare.com
2. **Or Email Login**: Enter your email for simple access
3. **Browse Events**: See all available events
4. **Purchase**: Select event → choose tier → complete payment
5. **View Tickets**: All your tickets in "My Tickets" section
6. **QR Entry**: Show QR code at event entry (auto-generates)

---

## 🎪 For Event Organizers (Provider App)

### Quick Setup
1. Visit the provider app URL in your mobile browser
2. Create your account (email or wallet)
3. **Create Event**:
   - Event name, description, date, venue
   - Add ticket tiers (name, price, quantity)
   - Configure resale settings (optional)
4. **Manage Tickets**: View sales, monitor inventory
5. **Scan Tickets**: Use built-in QR scanner at event entry

### At Event Day
1. Open Provider app
2. Navigate to your event
3. Tap "Start Scanning"
4. Point camera at attendee QR codes
5. ✅ Instant validation - entry granted!

## 🔧 Technical Requirements

### Minimum Requirements
- **Browser**: Safari 15+, Chrome 90+
- **Internet**: Required (no offline mode yet)
- **Wallet**: Phantom/Solflare for crypto payments (optional)

### Payment Options
- **Credit/Debit Card** (via Stripe)
- **Solana (SOL)** - if enabled by organizer
- **USDC** - stablecoin option if enabled

---

## 🎟️ How It Works

1. **Buy**: Purchase ticket → NFT minted to your wallet
2. **Transfer**: Gift/sell ticket via app (if organizer allows)
3. **Resell**: List on marketplace (with organizer's resale rules)
4. **Entry**: Show QR code → scanned at door
5. **After Event**: Keep as collectible or resell for future events

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Keep Your Wallet Secure**: Your tickets are NFTs in your wallet
- **Screenshot Backup**: QR codes can be screenshot for backup
- **No Lost Tickets**: Wallet recovery = ticket recovery
- **Organizer Rules**: Resale/transfer limits set by event organizer

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**"Wallet not connecting"**: Make sure Phantom/Solflare app is installed
**"QR not scanning"**: Increase screen brightness, clean camera lens
**"Ticket not showing"**: Check wallet address matches purchase
**"Payment failed"**: Try different payment method, check funds
